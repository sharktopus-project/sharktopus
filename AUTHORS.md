# Authors

sharktopus originated at the **Instituto de Estudos do Mar Almirante Paulo
Moreira (IEAPM)**, part of the Brazilian Navy's research directorate, to
support numerical weather prediction workflows for the WRF model. It is
distributed as a community-owned open-source project — anyone may contribute,
and credit is given in this file.
